About the Founder

Hi, I'm Dheeraj.

I'm a Telugu filmmaker, storyteller, and creator who has spent years figuring out how to make films without a big budget or a film school degree.

I built Filmiveda because I kept seeing talented Telugu filmmakers struggle with the same problems I had — not because they lacked talent, but because nobody gave them the right tools and knowledge.

My Story

Why I started Filmiveda

When I started making films, I had no mentor, no contacts, and very little money. I spent years learning things the hard way — wasting money on mistakes that someone else had already made.

I went to every filmmaking event, read every book I could find, and spent thousands of hours experimenting. Eventually, things started clicking. I figured out how to write better scripts, plan cheaper productions, and market films without a big budget.

"I don't want Telugu filmmakers to waste the years I wasted figuring this out alone."

That's why I started Filmiveda. Not as a big company — but as one filmmaker sharing what he knows with others who are on the same journey. Practical knowledge, honest advice, and affordable tools — that's what Filmiveda is built on.

Every e-book, every prompt pack, every workshop I run — it's all built from real experience, not theory. And every week I write The Wednesday Newsletter, I try to give you something genuinely useful you can apply that same day.
